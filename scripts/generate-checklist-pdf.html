Before You Leave Home

Download the NFL OnePass app and register (required for free entry)
Book your hotel (prices are only going up)
Pre-book parking on SpotHero if driving. Or skip it. ALL T rides are free.
Download Ready2Ride app for $25 Draft Pass (7 days unlimited PRT + buses)
Check road closure map. Bridges and I-279 exits are closed.
Charge your portable battery pack
Check weather forecast for Pittsburgh April 22-25
Save park-and-ride directions for your approach
Make dinner reservations (everywhere will be packed)

What to Pack

Must-Haves

Clear bag (12x6x12 for Stadium, 16x16x6 for Point State Park). $8 on Amazon.
Portable phone charger. OnePass drains battery. 500K people killing cell signal.
Comfortable walking shoes. 5-10 miles per day between venues.
Layers. April in Pittsburgh can be 45° or 75°.
Rain jacket or poncho. Outdoor event. April showers are real.
Phone with OnePass installed and account set up

Smart Additions

Sunscreen, sunglasses, hat
Water bottle (refill stations available)
Snacks. Food lines will be long.
Cash. Some vendors are cash-only.
Team jersey or gear
Small umbrella for clear bag

ALL T Rides FREE April 23-25 (Sheetz + PRT)

From North: Ross Park & Ride (I-279 Exit 5), Football Flyer bus, 25 min
From South: South Hills Village T Station, ride the T free, 35 min
From East: Wilkinsburg Park & Ride, P1 bus, 15 min
From West/Airport: PRT Route 28X, 45-60 min
From Butler County: Zelienople Rt 528 park-and-ride, Butler Transit every 30 min ($10 RT, exact cash)
Sheetz covers all fares on the T (red, blue, silver lines) and Monongahela Incline. Park at any T station, ride downtown for $0.
Scam Warning (BBB): Watch out for fake tickets, counterfeit merchandise, and rental fraud. The NFL Draft is FREE. If someone is selling tickets, it's a scam.

Money-Saving Tips

The draft is FREE (register on OnePass)
ALL T rides free. Skip $125-250/day parking.
$25 Draft Pass = 7 days unlimited (Ready2Ride)
Clear bag on Amazon ($8) not at event ($25)
Bring water and snacks. $15 beers add up.
Cranberry hotels: $120-180 vs $500-2K downtown
Walk from venues before calling Uber (surge)
Saturday = smallest crowds, best vibe
